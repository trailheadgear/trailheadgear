❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is budget gear actually good enough?？

Yes. The bar for camping gear is surprisingly low. A tent that does not leak + a warm bag + a soft pad = 90% of the experience. A $50 Coleman keeps rain out as well as a $300 tent. If you are car camping, that extra $250 barely matters.

Q: If I can only upgrade one thing?？

睡垫。帐篷可以凑合，睡袋可以多穿衣服，但睡不好=整个旅程毁了。投资一个好睡垫（$150-200）是你能为自己做的最能改善露营体验的单笔消费。

Q: 能不能比 $200 更便宜？

Yes — buy used. Facebook Marketplace and REI Used Gear can get you set up for under $100. Skip used sleeping bags (hygiene). A used tent that does not leak is a steal.

Q: How long will budget gear last?？

Coleman tents: 3-5 years. BRS stove: 1-2 years (ultralight = fragile). Foam pad: 5-10 years. The most disposable item is the $17 stove — which also happens to be the cheapest to replace.
