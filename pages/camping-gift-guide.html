❓ Gift Guide FAQ

Q: What is the safest gift when I do not know what gear they own?

Consumables. Socks, water purification tablets, fuel canisters, energy bars. Outdoor people are constantly burning through these. There is no such thing as "I already have one."

Q: What should I absolutely NOT buy?

Tents and sleeping bags — unless you know the exact model they want. These are deeply personal. Size, temperature rating, weight — only they can judge. If you want to go big, ask first.

Q: What is the best gift that costs nothing?

Go camping with them. Tell them: "I will come with you. You pick the spot, I will handle the food." To an outdoor person, this means more than any piece of gear.
