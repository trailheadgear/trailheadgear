📖 Backpack Buying Guide

What volume do you need?

Day hikes: 20-35L | 1-3 nights: 40-55L | 3-7 nights: 55-70L | Winter/expedition: 70L+

Fit matters more than specs

Every torso is different. Try packs on in-store if possible, or buy from retailers with good return policies. Osprey and Gregory offer the widest size ranges.
