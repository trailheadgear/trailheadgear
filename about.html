🏕️ About Trailhead Gear

Est. 2025 | Independent camping gear reviews

Why We Started

Buying camping gear online is exhausting. Thousands of products. Hundreds of conflicting reviews. Every brand claims to be "the best." We started Trailhead Gear to cut through the noise. One honest recommendation at a time.

How We Test Gear

We do not review products from an office chair. Every piece of gear we recommend has been:

  • Tested outdoors in real conditions — rain, wind, mud, cold, and heat
  • Compared head-to-head against competitors — not just "this is good" but "this is better than X because..."
  • Cross-checked with long-term owner reviews — from Amazon, REI, Reddit, and outdoor forums
  • Used for at least 2-3 nights per product — a quick unboxing is not a review

How We Make Money

Trailhead Gear is reader-supported. When you buy something through our links, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. We are an Amazon Associate and may also participate in other affiliate programs.

We never accept brand sponsorships, paid placements, or free products in exchange for favorable reviews. Our recommendations are based solely on testing and research. If a product is on our list, it earned its spot.

Why You Can Trust Us

  • No sponsored reviews. Ever. We buy or borrow every product we test.
  • We show you what we did NOT pick — and why. Transparency builds trust.
  • We update our guides regularly — prices change, new products launch, and we adjust.
  • If a product we recommended starts getting bad reviews, we remove it.
