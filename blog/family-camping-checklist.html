👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Camping: The Complete Guide

Updated June 2025 | 8 min read

Camping with kids is nothing like camping as a couple. But seeing your child look up at a sky full of stars for the first time — worth every bit of extra effort. This guide turns potential chaos into family memories.

Camping with kids — lower expectations, pack more snacks.

Kid-Friendly Campground Criteria

  • Flush toilets — Non-negotiable with potty-training toddlers
  • Running water — Kids get dirty. Repeatedly.
  • Nearby activities — Short trails, creek, playground
  • Within 30 min of a hospital — Just in case
  • Shade — Kids overheat faster than adults

Try: KOA campgrounds, state park family sites, Hipcamp "family friendly."

Family-Specific Gear

⛺ Tent: Go Big

Formula: rated capacity minus 2 = real comfort. Family of 4 = 6-person tent. Coleman Sundome 6 ($130, 100 sq ft). Bring a small play tent for the kids too.

🛏️ Sleeping Pads

  • Preschoolers: Foam pads — cannot leak, can jump on them.
  • Older kids: Inflatable pad + kids sleeping bag.
  • Never share one pad. Nobody sleeps.

🍳 Cooking: Simplify

  • Pre-made food — heat and eat.
  • Dual-burner stove: Camp Chef Everest 2X
  • One large cooler for food, one small for drinks.

Camp Activities for Kids

  • Scavenger hunt: Pinecone, clover, bird feather, smooth rock.
  • S'mores: Let them roast and build their own.
  • Stargazing: SkyView app — teach constellations.
  • Story time: Nature picture book.
  • Free play — the best activity of all.
